Get a permanent resident card

A permanent resident (PR) card shows that you’re a permanent resident of Canada. You need it to return to Canada after travelling.

Find out how to submit an application to

  • get your first PR card
  • renew an expired or expiring card
  • report or replace a lost card
  • update the information on your card, such as your name

Processing times

Processing times will vary based on

  • the type of application submitted
  • if the application is complete
  • how quickly we expect to process applications we’ve already received
  • how easily we can verify your information
  • how long you take to respond to any requests or concerns
  • other factors
